What is pinkgill?

Pinkgill is an experimental ATProto site to experiment with a new, more powerful kind of content for social media or social-like things. Essentially, it's a way of posting arbitrary interactive content that is nevertheless safe (and can be made privacy-friendly, at least if you trust your PDS — which you kind of have to, that's the point of a user agent).

Additionally, tiles can declare special kinds of actions that they know how to handle (wishes), and these can help them do powerful things — notably generating more tiles. This means that you can create new social media formats with just some HTML, CSS, and JS.

If you're wondering why it's called "pinkgill" when all things AT are either blue or atmospheric, it's because several kinds of pinkgill mushrooms are indeed blue, notably the sky-blue mushroom. And mushrooms are cool.
